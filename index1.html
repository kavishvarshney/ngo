OUR STORY

We respect values and believe in giving and sharing to society. That is the main reason why we started our NGO. My parents taught us to give back to the community and to be optimistic about the future.From childhood, I saw how my father helped out in our local communities, and we were taught that anything is possible with giving and sharing. Unfortunately, some outliers make it hard for some people to reach their potential: things like when they were born, limitations of who their parents are, Culture, where they grew up, and the environment in which they have lived their childhood.
My parents thought to use resources and my skills to create a world where everyone has the opportunity to lead a healthy and happy life. Most importantly, we believe this: All lives are valuable, and they have a fundamental need to live a healthy and happy life.

The challenge we always have was how to do that in a meaningful, right and create high-impact. Our NGO was drawn to things that sprang from experiences throughout, so we began donating supplies to the poor across Manthani to have everyone eat three meals daily. As we traveled more, we also became curious about inequalities further from home. We started helping them when they are sick. We built a hospital to treat the patients with a nominal amount. We began consulting experts in each village, learning from locals where we wanted to work and help and poverty more deeply with our initiatives.

We provided employment for youth by starting IT company and other small employment, local business opportunities as we can. We also provided education to the underprivileged youth by providing financial help. We donated books, several PCS, gym equipment, and provide sports facilities in the schools. We tried to make sure that every student had an equal opportunity to learn, graduate, and succeed. We are trying new ways and plan activities out how we might use our voices to raise the visibility of health and education and how our resources could start saving and transforming the lives of the poor.

Sports

We are focused on the areas of dire need in rural areas of Telangana state, on ways in which we can benefit the poor.
Sunil Reddy Chandrupatla's passion for sports supports potential athletes to achieve their dreams and reach the pinnacle in their respective sport, mainly by providing the equipment to the schools, maintaining the grounds, creating awareness of fitness. Sunil's ambitions for better prospects in sports and fitness have grown beyond the sports arena to creating a better future for the rural Youth of our state. He is committed to building youth-focused initiatives, which connect children to the right resources and use his partnerships to set up support services to the vulnerable section of our state.

Social Service

The goal of our NGO initiative is to create opportunities for thousands of Youth in rural areas. Several initiatives were created in response to mounting evidence that Youth worldwide has been facing critical challenges, including an alarming gap between those with access to the infrastructure, finance, skillset, and opportunities to succeed in rural areas.
With our NGO, Chandrupatla Ansuyamma Trust hoped to touch the lives of Youth in rural areas Mobilizing Youth to help realize their potential, particularly in education, hygiene, health, employment, and entrepreneurship.
Providing Platform youth, women, and other businesses to get involved and personally offer short term or long term employment, recruitment drives, awareness to continue the education without stopping in the middle Reaching and engaging Helping hands to start a small business, pursue the education and stand on their feet to be the pillars and strength of the families

Health Care

Our comprehensive health program on NGOs focuses on the early detection of diseases and providing timely treatment to the poor and underprivileged. These efforts save lives and promise better futures for the families' well-being. Sometimes many people in rural areas die due to significant diseases. They lack support from people and society. They suffer adverse effects, and it's unfortunate to see their lives during that stage. They cannot afford any medical treatment due to financial constraints.
We also provide financial help to people with disabilities. Our goal is not only to provide financial support. Through our programs, we also offer aid & equipment, in which he will be able to do the daily work efficiently.
Necessary medical treatment, medicines, and other essential things will be provided by our NGO, which will be helpful to these victims rehabilitate their lives. Also, they will have hope and positivity
