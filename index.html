About Us

Our NGO Chandrupatla Anasuyamma Trust is a NEXT GENERATION HELPING HANDS SOCIETY based out of Manthani in Telangana. Our Foundation aims for the betterment of the rural areas in Telangana by catering to the needs of underprivileged children, youth from socially and economically disadvantaged backgrounds and empowering the youth and inspiring volunteerism. We thrive on creating an equitable world for everyone regardless of gender, class, ethnicity, location, religious and sexual orientation by empowering the most marginalized. Our Foundation aims to use education, health care, sports development, and women empowerment of rural areas as a aim to break through the cycle of poverty, create opportunities for income, growth, and sustainable development.

Our Story

Lack of access to health care and hygiene has negative long-term impacts on individuals and communities. Low-income families in rural areas are more likely to experience poor health outcomes. Providing good quality, affordable, preventive care targeted to uninsured and low-income patients health care facilities confronts these disparities, eliminating the societal and personal cost of damaging health problems and enabling all people to achieve healthy, successful lives. Underinsured and uninsured populations have increased difficulty accessing medical care due to cost and experience worsened health outcomes. Access to affordable, accessible medical care means that families go without the information, resources, and treatment they need for healthy lives. Our facilities and our teams Provide quality, affordable medical services to thousands of patients who otherwise would not have access to care.
